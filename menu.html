    • Subnautica Café Menu

    Welcome to the Subnautica Café, where the flavours of the deep sea meet the art of culinary crafting. Our menu is inspired by the diverse marine life and enchanting landscapes of the underwater world. Dive into a culinary adventure unlike any other.

    Beverages:

    Lava Geyser Espresso: A bold and invigorating espresso blend, inspired by the fiery landscapes of Subnautica. Served with a hint of cinnamon for an extra kick.

    Jelly Shroom Latte: A smooth latte infused with the sweet and savory flavours of jelly shrooms. Topped with whipped cream and a sprinkle of edible glitter for an extra touch of magic.

    Crashfish Cappuccino: A creamy cappuccino with a fiery twist, featuring a dash of chili powder and caramel syrup. Guaranteed to awaken your senses and leave you craving more.

    Reefback Mocha: Indulge in the rich flavours of chocolate and coconut with our signature reefback mocha. Served with a dollop of whipped cream and a drizzle of caramel sauce for a decadent treat.

    Pastries and Snacks:

    Coral Tube Donuts: Fluffy donuts infused with tropical fruit flavours and coated in a colourful coral tube glaze. A delightful snack to satisfy your sweet cravings.

    Kelp Forest Croissants: Light and buttery croissants filled with savory ingredients such as smoked salmon, cream cheese, and fresh herbs. Perfect for a quick bite on the go.

    Barnacle Biscotti: Crunchy biscotti studded with toasted almonds and dried cranberries, reminiscent of the barnacles that cling to the rocky surfaces of Subnautica.

    Seaglide Energy Bars: Nutrient-rich energy bars packed with oats, nuts, and dried fruits. Ideal for refuelling after a long day of exploring the depths.

    Specialty Drinks:

    Aurora Sunrise Smoothie: A refreshing blend of mango, pineapple, and coconut milk, reminiscent of the vibrant hues of the Aurora spaceship. Served with a splash of grenadine for a sunrise-inspired twist.

    Cyclops Cooler: Cool off with our signature Cyclops cooler, a fruity concoction featuring blended strawberries, bananas, and orange juice. Topped with a pineapple wedge and a paper umbrella for a touch of tropical flair.

    Ghost Leviathan Lemonade: Quench your thirst with our zesty ghost leviathan lemonade, made with freshly squeezed lemons and a hint of mint. Served over ice for a refreshing burst of flavour.

    Peeper Potion: A magical elixir infused with blue curaçao, lemon-lime soda, and a splash of coconut rum. Garnished with a sugared rim and a glowing peeper garnish for an enchanting experience.

    Kids' Menu:

    Peeper Nuggets: Bite-sized chicken nuggets served with a side of seaweed fries and kelp slaw. A kid-friendly option for young adventurers.

    Seamoth Sandwich: A mini submarine sandwich filled with turkey, cheese, lettuce, and tomato. Served with carrot sticks and ranch dipping sauce for a balanced meal.

    Bubble Blower Smoothie: A fruity smoothie made with strawberries, bananas, and vanilla yogurt. Served with a fun bubble blower toy for added entertainment.

    Indulge your senses and embark on a culinary journey through the depths of Subnautica at the Subnautica Café. We look forward to serving you!
